About Us

ALOD, “A Livin’ of Design” represents the belief in our design. Identity and Brand design are like searching for the North Star. The studio was formed to bring our belief into our creative process and find the unique & long-lasting element of design (the North Star Phenom) that truly represents the company.

From Naming to Brand Positioning to Identity System and Logo Creation, we are experts in creating unique and compelling visual identities. These include all key elements across the mediums that embody the DNA of a successful brand.

Our approach is simple. Communicate, align, communicate and align. Yes. We do not create an identity for you, we create an identity that is you. Communicating your thought, belief, industry insight, feeling, and most importantly your trust in us is the key to the greatest result.
