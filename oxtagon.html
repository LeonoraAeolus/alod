Type of Work:

Visual Identity

Partner: Aces Chew

Overview

Oxtagon is a new form advisory agency focusing on Trading and Investment. ALOD was assigned to design a logo that represents the Chinese elite advisor team. The origin of the name was inspired by the Octagon which is also represented by 8. Through different variations, the logo was brought to life utilizing the sides of Octagon into a mirroring position of Chinese “8”.
